A Maker's Guide to I2C How the bus works, and what to hang off of it.

I2C fundamentals, the Pico API, 84 native busses on a single Pico, and a dual-redundant EEPROM data store. Companion files will appear here when Volume 3 ships.

Topology, addressing, command patterns, and how to read the data sheets.

CH 4 I2C Software on a Pico

The SDK functions, the nostop parameter, and device enumeration.

CH 5 84 I2C Busses

Crosspoint busses from pin-pair combinations — no bus expanders required. Includes the 18-sensor HTU-31D test board.

CH 6 EEPROM Devices

The 24C256 family, pageification, and a dual-redundant CRC-protected database.
