Volume 2

A Maker's Guide to Light Emitting Projects Projects with lights. Lots of lights.

Clocks, displays, cubes, games, and the libraries that hold them together. Companion files will appear here when Volume 2 ships.

Volume 2 is in production. The chapter list below is subject to change until release.

The 156-LED round clock, and the 247-LED version with a moon phase complication.

CH 4 The Cube

8 × 8 and 16 × 16 five-sided LED cubes.

CH 5 The Dot Matrix Display

The 8 × 8 “nixie tube style” display.

CH 6 HEXBUS Game

A hexagonal-grid logic puzzle on LED hardware.

CH 7 Instrumentation Panel

Putting the lights to work.

CH 8 KiCad Move (km)

A utility for wrangling PCB layouts.

CH 9 GPS Library

Time synchronization for the clocks.

APP A The WS2812 LED

The addressable LED that powers most of this volume.
