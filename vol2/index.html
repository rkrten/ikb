The Iron Krten Bench — Volume 2: A Maker's Guide to Light Emitting Projects

This volume builds projects with lots of LEDs:

  • 156 LED round clock
  • 247 LED round clock with moon phase complication
  • 8 x 8 "nixie tube style" display
  • 8 x 8 and 16 x 16 5-sided cube

The projects are based on the Raspberry Pico processor.
