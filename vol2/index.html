Raspberry Pi Pico LED Projects Dial Clocks, LED Cube, Dot Matrix Display, and HEXBUS Game

Projects with lights. Lots of lights.

Each chapter below links to its companion files. Gerber zips are ready to upload to JLCPCB as-is; KiCad zips contain the full schematic and PCB project; source zips contain the C code, Makefiles, and anything else the chapter walks through.

8 × 8 and 16 × 16 five-sided LED cubes.
